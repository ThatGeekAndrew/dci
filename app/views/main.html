Using the emulator

This is your remote. There are many like it, but this one is yours! Use this to control your TV emulator. We took special care to ensure that all buttons we mapped are mapped accurately.

Your remote won't do anything that the customers wont. All relevant buttons will be highlighted for easy use. Please note that not every button will be usable on every menu regardless if they're highlighted.
